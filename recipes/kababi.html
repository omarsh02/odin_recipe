KABABI

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2kg white rice
  • 2 midium sized oninons
  • 2 garlic strips
  • salt, black pepper, cumin

Steps:

  1. crush rice into semi fine powder, to make blending easier later
  2. blend rice, meat, onions and garlic into a heavy green paste, that will be the base of the Kababi
  3. add seasoning and give a quick blend
  4. fry some of the meat on low heat to use as filling for the kababi
  5. form paste into an ellipse shape, and add fried meat in center
  6. throw kababi in boiling water WITHOUT stirring, only stir after water has started boiling again, for 15 minutes
  7. fry in oil after boiling
  8. la3alo yotmor
